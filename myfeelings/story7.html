DEPRESSION

Keep Yourself Busy, And There Will Be No Time For Depression.

Depression — అవసాదం — अवसाद

A word that sounds small when spoken... but feels unbelievably heavy when carried.

"Once upon a time..."

No... Wait...

Let's start in a different way.

Generally, people fall into depression when they lose someone they love... when death steals a person they were never ready to let go of.

Some lose themselves after being betrayed by the people they trusted the most.

Some after broken relationships.

Some after losing their dignity, their reputation, their dreams, or after facing the greatest failure of their lives.

Life suddenly... stops.

The world keeps moving... but somewhere inside, they don't.

The truth is... life quietly asks us to begin again, to restart, to rebuild ourselves from the ruins.

But... How?

How do you move on when your heart still waits for someone who is gone?
How do you imagine life without them?
How do you walk forward when your feet are still standing in yesterday?
How do you face reality... when reality itself hurts?

And then... the memories arrive.

The places...
The favourites...
The late-night conversations...
The laughs...
The pain...
The support...
The moments they stood beside you...

Suddenly, your happiest memories become your heaviest wounds.

Every little thing... starts hitting hard.

And depression slowly whispers...

"Stay here... Don't move."

But life whispers something else...

"You still have a chance."

Men or women... strength is not about never crying.

Strength is crying, breaking, feeling completely lost... and still choosing to stand up.

Yes... it is hard.

Very hard...

I understand.

Because... I have lived through it too.

My Story

I got depressed when I was broken.

I got depressed when I failed to get a job.

I got depressed when I felt I had lost my family's trust in me.

There were nights when I cried.

Days when I felt completely helpless.

Moments when I believed I was totally alone.

No one beside me.
No one to understand me.

And then... one day...

I asked myself...
"Why can't I be with myself... when I am alone?"

Maybe... no one stood beside me.

But... I stood beside myself.

I believed in myself.

I trusted myself.

Slowly... I realised...

"I am not alone.

I have Me."

So... I Changed.

I changed my thinking.

I changed the way I spoke.

I changed my attitude.

I changed my behaviour.

Not because the world asked me to.

But because...

I wanted to be Happy.

And I made one promise to myself...

"I will be Happy."

This Is Not The End.

This pain... is not your final chapter.

This heartbreak...

This failure...

This loneliness...

This darkness...

Maybe...

Just maybe...

It is your second chance to restart life.

A new version of You is waiting on the other side of pain.

So... cry if you need to.

Feel sad if you must.

Take your time.

But... don't stop living.

Keep Moving.
Keep Growing.
Keep Trying.

And most importantly...

Keep yourself Busy, because Depression feeds on empty silence.
Depression... doesn't disappear overnight.

Healing is not about forgetting the past.

Healing is about learning to live with it without letting it control your future.

Some days will still feel heavy.

Some nights will still hurt.

But every small step you take today is taking you closer to the person you are becoming.

Never be ashamed to ask for help.

Never think you're weak for sharing your pain.

Sometimes, the bravest thing you can do is simply say,

"I need help."

And if no one is around...

Start with yourself.

Believe in yourself.

Stand beside yourself.

Because... before anyone else, you deserve your own love, your own support, and your own hope.

Depression — Keep yourself busy, and one day, you won't have time for it anymore.
