Unwritten Destinies

Journey with a Beautiful Stranger in the Midnight Express

Two souls scripted to meet but not to stay — love meant to be felt, not lived.

Love, mystery, destiny, and a fleeting connection under moonlight.
Date: 20/07/2025 Time: 9:05 p.m. Route: Hyderabad → Chennai

It was around 9 PM, the quiet hum of the night settling around me. Beside my berth, there she lay sleeping—so effortlessly beautiful.
Just a fleeting view of her serene presence sparked a soft emotion inside me; the grace of her presence, the quiet rhythm of her breathing, and the delicate charm of her feet seemed to reach straight to my heart, like a soft breeze whispering to a silent shore—adorned with an ankle chain that mesmerized me with every sparkle and whisper.
A tiny nose ring graced her face, adding a sparkle of elegance, making her beauty feel timeless. She seemed like a sweet, innocent dream wrapped in serenity—her sleeping face reflecting a soft, unguarded charm.
In my mother tongue, I would say:

"అందమే అసూయపడేలా ఉంది, ఆమె అందం."

Her lips—like petals kissed by the morning dew—left me with an unexplainable feeling, somewhere between admiration and wonder.
A soft, charming mole rested on her lips, like a little beauty mark, adding even more grace to her already beautiful face. Her cuteness, her innocence, and those mesmerizing eyes… words fall short; they cannot capture the purity of what I felt in that quiet moment.
So stunning, her beauty gentle and rare,
Her presence delicately stays in the midnight air.
No, this isn’t love. I don’t believe in the madness of love at first sight.
But I do believe in beauty.
I do believe in admiration.
And in that moment,
I knew she was simply… awesome.

— Harsha Vinjamuri
